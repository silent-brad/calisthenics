Who Am I?

I'm a passionate advocate for bodyweight training and functional movement. This blog documents my journey through calisthenics, sharing techniques, progressions, and insights I've learned along the way.

My Philosophy

"The body achieves what the mind believes. Through consistent practice and progressive overload, we can unlock incredible strength using nothing but our own bodyweight."

I believe in the power of simplicity - that the most effective training comes from mastering fundamental movements and progressively challenging ourselves with variations and increased difficulty.
